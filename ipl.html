Portfolio — Case Study

IPL Season
Ad Concepts

Three ad concepts for a hair care brand timed around IPL season. Two for men's, one for women's. Same product brief, different angles, different appeals.

Project Overview

IPL season is one of the most watched sporting events in India and one of the most contested advertising windows of the year. Every brand wants to be in the conversation. The brief here was sharper than that: find a way for a hair care brand to belong in that conversation without forcing the connection.

Both categories use a humorous, light-hearted register.

Men's Category

Two variations on the same brief. The explicit version makes the product the subject from the start. The subtle version withholds it until the payoff, letting the situation do the work for longer. Both are for Brisa's silk-hair range.

Women's Category

A single concept built around a different mechanic: envy appeal routed through social media. The women's IPL category has a growing and genuinely engaged audience. The brief was to make an ad that felt native to that world: one that uses social media's grammar without borrowing its worst habits.
